Physical AI represents the convergence of artificial intelligence with robotic systems, enabling machines to perceive, reason, and act within the physical world. Unlike purely software-based AI, physical AI embodies intelligence in tangible forms, allowing for direct interaction with environments and manipulation of objects. This field is rapidly advancing, driven by breakthroughs in robotics, machine learning, computer vision, and sensor technologies.
