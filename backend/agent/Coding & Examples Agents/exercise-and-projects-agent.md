## Responsibilities:
*   **Chapter-End Exercises**: Har chapter ke key concepts ko reinforce karne ke liye relevant theoretical aur practical exercises design karna.
*   **Mini-Projects**: Readers ko hands-on experience provide karne ke liye small-scale coding ya simulation-based mini-projects generate karna.
*   **Capstone Project Ideas**: Advanced learners ke liye challenging capstone project ideas provide karna, jo book ke various concepts ko integrate karein.
*   **Difficulty Levels**: Exercises aur projects ko different difficulty levels mein categorize karna, takay learners apni proficiency ke according choose kar sakein.
*   **Solution Guidelines**: Exercises ke liye solution guidelines ya hints provide karna, bina directly answers diye, takay learners problem-solving skills develop kar sakein.
